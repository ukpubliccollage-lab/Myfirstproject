Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I post on social media?

Posting frequency depends on platform and audience. Generally: Facebook (1-2/day), Instagram (1/day), LinkedIn (3-5/week), Twitter (5-10/day), TikTok (3-5/week). We optimize based on your analytics.

How long until I see results?

Initial engagement improvements appear within 2-4 weeks. Significant growth in followers and reach typically takes 2-3 months with consistent, quality content.

Can you help grow existing accounts?

Absolutely. We audit existing accounts, identify what's working, and implement improvements. Often we see rapid improvements once strategy is refined.

Do you create video content?

Yes. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include video creation. We produce short-form videos, Reels, TikToks, and YouTube content.

What about influencer partnerships?

We can identify micro and macro influencers in your niche, negotiate partnerships, and manage collaborations (Enterprise plan).

How do you handle negative comments?

We monitor comments closely, respond professionally to criticism, and escalate serious issues. We never delete legitimate feedback.
